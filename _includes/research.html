Research

I am interested in problems that involve modeling large amounts of uncertain data.

Before joining UTD, I was a Master’s student at Indiana University. I worked with Prof. David Crandall on bayesian uncertainty quantification algorithms for deep neural networks. I completed my M.S. in May 2020.

As an undergraduate student at Vellore Institute of Technology, I worked with Prof. Daphne Lopez on deep models for image caption generation and chatbots.

For my publications, see my page on Google Scholar.
